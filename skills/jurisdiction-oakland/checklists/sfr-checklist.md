# Oakland SFR Submittal Checklist (2022 CBC/CRC)

Jurisdiction: City of Oakland, CA
Applicable code cycle: 2022 California Building Standards Code (Title 24), effective January 1, 2023
Local amendments: Oakland Municipal Code (OMC) Title 15 — Buildings and Construction
Permit type: Single-Family Residential (SFR) — new construction and additions

Oakland-specific notes for the QuestionChecklistAgent:
- Oakland Planning clearance (Planning Bureau sign-off on zoning) is required BEFORE a
  Building permit can be issued for new construction and most additions.
- EBMUD (East Bay Municipal Utility District) water availability and fire-flow letter
  is required for new construction.
- Oakland Fire Prevention Bureau (OFP) concurrent review is required for new SFR
  projects with NFPA 13D fire sprinkler systems.
- Oakland Energy Reach Code sets a higher bar than the state minimum for new SFR:
  all-electric ready panel (200A minimum), no gas piping in new SFR effective
  January 1, 2023 (OMC 15.01.010).

---

## 1. Pre-Application / Administrative

- **OAK-ADM-001**: Planning Bureau clearance letter or approval stamp obtained prior to Building permit issuance (all-electric, zoning compliance confirmed).
- **OAK-ADM-002**: EBMUD water availability and fire-flow availability letter included with application for new SFR.
- **OAK-ADM-003**: Asbestos/lead-paint survey included for projects involving demolition of pre-1978 structures (Oakland HazMat requirement).
- **OAK-ADM-004**: Historic preservation review or exemption letter included if property is in a historic district or is a potential historic resource.
- **OAK-ADM-005**: School fees (OUSD developer fee) calculation and receipt included with application.

---

## 2. Site Documents

- **SITE-001**: Plot plan provided showing property lines, existing and proposed structures, setbacks, north arrow, scale, APN, and project address.
- **SITE-002**: Setback dimensions labeled and compliant with Zoning Chapter 17.102 (RM, RS zones) and any Planning approval conditions.
- **SITE-003**: Impervious surface area and stormwater management approach shown (Oakland's Municipal Regional Permit Stormwater requirements apply for new construction ≥ 2,500 sq ft disturbed area).
- **SITE-004**: Grading and drainage plan provided for projects with > 50 cy cut/fill or > 15% slope.
- **SITE-005**: Soils report included for hillside projects (slopes > 15%), expansive soils areas, or when required by Building Official.
- **SITE-006**: Utility connections shown: water meter, sewer lateral, gas (only for existing/retrofit), and electrical service point.

---

## 3. Foundation

- **FNDN-001**: Foundation plan at minimum 1/4" = 1'-0" scale provided.
- **FNDN-002**: Footing width, depth, and reinforcement specified per CBC Section 1806 / CRC R403.
- **FNDN-003**: Slab thickness and reinforcement specified; minimum 3.5" per CRC R506.1.
- **FNDN-004**: Vapor barrier under slab specified: minimum 10-mil polyethylene per CRC R506.2.3 (Oakland enforces the stricter detail in hillside areas).
- **FNDN-005**: Anchor bolt layout shown: minimum 1/2" at 6 ft o.c. per CRC R403.1.6; additional anchors at shear wall boundary members.
- **FNDN-006**: Hold-down hardware identified and correlated with shear wall schedule.
- **FNDN-007**: Drainage provisions at foundation (French drain or perimeter drain) shown for hillside lots.

---

## 4. Framing / Structural

- **FRNG-001**: Floor plan at 1/4" = 1'-0" minimum scale with all rooms labeled and dimensioned.
- **FRNG-002**: Framing plan showing joist/rafter size, spacing, span, species, and grade.
- **FRNG-003**: Header schedule provided per CRC R602.7; engineered lumber headers identified by manufacturer and span table reference.
- **FRNG-004**: Shear wall schedule provided with panel type, nailing, hold-downs, and boundary nailing.
- **FRNG-005**: Bearing walls and load path to foundation identified.
- **FRNG-006**: Ceiling heights shown; minimum 7'-0" for habitable rooms per CRC R305.1.
- **FRNG-007**: Stair dimensions: minimum 36" clear width, max 7-3/4" riser, min 10" tread nosing per CRC R311.7.
- **FRNG-008**: Guard height shown: 36" at stairs, 42" at balconies/decks > 30" above grade per CRC R312.
- **FRNG-009**: Attic access opening shown: minimum 22" x 30" per CRC R807.1.
- **FRNG-010**: Lateral analysis or prescriptive braced-wall compliance documented per Oakland's Seismic Hazard Zone (Zone 4 CBC/ASCE 7 criteria).

---

## 5. Architectural / Egress

- **ARCH-001**: All rooms labeled with function and dimensions.
- **ARCH-002**: Egress windows in all sleeping rooms showing NCO dimensions; minimum 5.7 sq ft NCO, 20" wide, 24" high, max 44" sill per CRC R310.2.1.
- **ARCH-003**: Door schedule with tag, size, fire rating, hardware, and swing; egress doors minimum 32" net clear per CRC R311.2.
- **ARCH-004**: Window schedule with tag, size, U-factor, SHGC, and NCO dimensions.
- **ARCH-005**: Smoke alarms located in each sleeping room, outside each sleeping area, and on each story per CRC R314.3; hardwired with battery backup for new construction.
- **ARCH-006**: Carbon monoxide alarms on each level with sleeping area and within 15 ft of each sleeping room per CRC R315.3.
- **ARCH-007**: Exit discharge path to public way shown.
- **ARCH-008**: Stairway handrail at 34"–38" above nosing, graspable profile, per CRC R311.7.8.

---

## 6. Mechanical (HVAC)

- **MECH-001**: HVAC equipment schedule showing type, capacity, and location.
- **MECH-002**: Heating load calculation (Manual J or Title 24 Part 6) included; no gas heating equipment in new SFR per Oakland Energy Reach Code.
- **MECH-003**: Duct routing and insulation specified per Title 24 Part 6.
- **MECH-004**: Bathroom exhaust fan(s) shown: minimum 50 cfm per CRC M1507.3.
- **MECH-005**: Kitchen exhaust hood rated minimum 100 cfm, ducted to exterior per CRC M1503.1.
- **MECH-006**: Whole-house mechanical ventilation shown per CRC M1505 / Title 24 Part 6 Section 150.0(o).
- **MECH-007**: All-electric mechanical systems only in new SFR (no gas appliances) per Oakland Energy Reach Code effective January 1, 2023.
- **MECH-008**: Heat pump HVAC system specified; COP and HSPF ratings noted.

---

## 7. Electrical

- **ELEC-001**: Electrical service size shown; minimum 200A panel for new SFR per Oakland Energy Reach Code (to support all-electric loads).
- **ELEC-002**: Panel schedule provided; load calculation included.
- **ELEC-003**: AFCI protection on all 120V 15A and 20A circuits per CEC Article 210.12.
- **ELEC-004**: GFCI protection at all required locations per CEC Article 210.8.
- **ELEC-005**: Tamper-resistant receptacles at all required locations per CEC 406.12.
- **ELEC-006**: EV-ready outlet or EVSE in garage/carport per Title 24 Part 6 Section 150.0(w); Oakland requires Level 2-ready (240V, 50A) per Reach Code.
- **ELEC-007**: Solar PV-ready conduit and dedicated panel space per Title 24 Part 6 Section 150.0(u).
- **ELEC-008**: Exterior lighting on occupancy sensors or timers per Title 24 Part 6 Section 150.0(k).
- **ELEC-009**: Smoke alarm interconnection method noted (hardwired, battery backup, all interconnected) per CRC R314.4.
- **ELEC-010**: No new gas service or gas piping shown for new SFR (Oakland all-electric mandate); confirm with Oakland Reach Code compliance checklist.

---

## 8. Plumbing

- **PLMB-001**: Plumbing plan or riser diagram showing DWV routing and fixture locations.
- **PLMB-002**: Water heater type: heat pump water heater required for new SFR per Oakland Energy Reach Code and Title 24 Part 6 Section 150.0(n).
- **PLMB-003**: Water heater seismic strapping shown per CPC Section 507.2.
- **PLMB-004**: Water meter and service size shown; EBMUD approval included.
- **PLMB-005**: Fixture unit count per CPC Appendix A Table A 2.1.
- **PLMB-006**: Shower valves pressure-balancing or thermostatic, max 120°F per CPC Section 408.4.
- **PLMB-007**: No gas piping shown in new SFR (all-electric mandate); only existing gas permitted in permitted additions/remodels per Oakland OMC.
- **PLMB-008**: Backflow prevention at hose bibbs and irrigation per CPC Section 603.

---

## 9. Energy (Title 24 Part 6 + Oakland Reach Code)

- **ENER-001**: Title 24 Part 6 compliance forms included: CF1R signed by energy consultant or HERS rater.
- **ENER-002**: Oakland Energy Reach Code compliance worksheet included (separate from state CF1R); confirms all-electric compliance and any additional local requirements.
- **ENER-003**: Insulation R-values on plans consistent with CF1R (walls, attic, floor/ceiling).
- **ENER-004**: Fenestration U-factor and SHGC consistent with CF1R and window schedule.
- **ENER-005**: Heat pump water heater specified meeting CEC efficiency requirements (UEF ≥ 3.75 for residential tank per 2022 code).
- **ENER-006**: Duct leakage testing required; HERS rater verification noted on CF1R.
- **ENER-007**: Cool roof or radiant barrier specified as required by CF1R compliance path.
- **ENER-008**: Battery storage system (10 kWh minimum) noted per Oakland Reach Code Section (where mandatory for new SFR construction).

---

## 10. Accessibility

- **ACCS-001**: Accessible route from street or parking to primary entrance shown.
- **ACCS-002**: Primary entrance threshold ≤ 1/2" change in level; exterior route slope ≤ 8.33% per CBC 11B-402.
- **ACCS-003**: If accessible parking provided, stall width minimum 9' plus 5' access aisle per CBC 11B-502.
- **ACCS-004**: Interior doorway widths minimum 32" net clear at primary accessible route per CBC 11B-404.

---

## 11. Fire and Life Safety

- **FIRE-001**: Automatic fire sprinkler system required per Oakland Fire Prevention Bureau policy for new SFR with gross floor area ≥ 3,600 sq ft, or as required by OFP concurrent review; NFPA 13D scope shown if applicable.
- **FIRE-002**: OFP concurrent fire plan review stamp obtained or concurrent review initiated; fire plan check fees paid.
- **FIRE-003**: Deferred submittal for fire sprinkler system documented on plan cover sheet.
- **FIRE-004**: Garage-to-dwelling separation shown: minimum 1/2" Type X gypsum board on garage side; 20-minute rated door per CRC R302.5.1.
- **FIRE-005**: WUI zone compliance documented if property is in Oakland's designated Wildland-Urban Interface area (many Oakland Hills parcels); Class A roof and ignition-resistant construction per CBC Chapter 7A.
- **FIRE-006**: Oakland Fire Prevention Bureau pre-inspection clearance for any deferred submittals noted in the permit application.

---

## 12. CALGreen (Title 24 Part 11)

- **CALG-001**: CALGreen mandatory measures checklist included with permit application.
- **CALG-002**: Construction waste diversion plan or documentation showing minimum 65% diversion per CALGreen Section 4.408.1.
- **CALG-003**: Low-VOC materials (adhesives, sealants, paints, carpets) specified per CALGreen Sections 4.504.2–4.504.3.
- **CALG-004**: Moisture management measures noted: flashing, vapor retarder per CALGreen Section 4.505.2.
- **CALG-005**: Whole-house mechanical ventilation rate per ASHRAE 62.2 or CRC M1505 per CALGreen Section 4.506.1.
- **CALG-006**: Water-conserving fixtures: toilets ≤ 1.28 gpf, lavatory faucets ≤ 1.2 gpm, showers ≤ 1.8 gpm per CALGreen Section 4.303.
- **CALG-007**: EV infrastructure per CALGreen Section 4.106.4 and Oakland Reach Code; panel capacity for Level 2 charging noted.
- **CALG-008**: Drought-tolerant landscaping compliant with MWELO and EBMUD water-use requirements for all landscaped areas ≥ 500 sq ft.
