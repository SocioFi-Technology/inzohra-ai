# Santa Rosa SFR Submittal Checklist (2022 CBC/CRC)

Jurisdiction: City of Santa Rosa, CA
Applicable code cycle: 2022 California Building Standards Code (Title 24), effective January 1, 2023
Permit type: Single-Family Residential (SFR) — new construction and additions
Primary contact: Santa Rosa Building and Code Enforcement Division

Each item below is phrased so the QuestionChecklistAgent can match it against plan-set entities
and code-KB retrievals. Items are tagged with a canonical `item_id` used in `checklist_queries`.

---

## 1. Site Documents

- **SITE-001**: Plot plan provided showing property lines, existing and proposed structures, setbacks to all property lines, north arrow, scale, APN, and project address.
- **SITE-002**: Setback dimensions shown to all property lines and labeled in compliance with zoning (front, rear, and side yard minimums per SR Municipal Code).
- **SITE-003**: Driveway, walkway, and impervious surface area identified on site plan.
- **SITE-004**: Grading and drainage plan provided when significant grading is proposed (cut or fill > 50 cy or > 5 ft of cut/fill).
- **SITE-005**: Soils report provided when required by Building Department (expansive soils, slope > 15%, or as flagged by Building Official per CBC Section 1803.1).
- **SITE-006**: Utility lines (water, sewer, gas, electrical) shown on site plan with point of connection to public main.

---

## 2. Foundation

- **FNDN-001**: Foundation plan provided at minimum 1/4" = 1'-0" scale.
- **FNDN-002**: Footing sizes, reinforcement, and embedment depth shown on plan or in general notes.
- **FNDN-003**: Slab thickness and reinforcement specified (minimum 3.5" per CRC R506.1).
- **FNDN-004**: Vapor barrier under slab-on-grade specified (minimum 6-mil polyethylene per CRC R506.2.3).
- **FNDN-005**: Anchor bolt size, spacing, and embedment shown (minimum 1/2" dia. at 6 ft o.c. per CRC R403.1.6).
- **FNDN-006**: Crawl space ventilation provided where applicable (CRC R408.1: min 1 sq ft per 150 sq ft of floor area).
- **FNDN-007**: Hold-down hardware and post bases identified where required by shear wall schedule.

---

## 3. Framing / Structural

- **FRNG-001**: Floor plan(s) provided at 1/4" = 1'-0" minimum scale with all rooms labeled and dimensioned.
- **FRNG-002**: Framing plan provided showing joist/rafter size, spacing, span, and species/grade.
- **FRNG-003**: Header schedule provided specifying header size and span for each opening (CRC R602.7).
- **FRNG-004**: Shear wall schedule provided with sheathing type, nailing pattern, hold-downs, and boundary members.
- **FRNG-005**: Bearing wall locations identified and correlated with foundation plan.
- **FRNG-006**: Point loads from above shown with load path traced to foundation.
- **FRNG-007**: Ceiling height dimensions shown on floor plan (minimum 7'-0" per CRC R305.1).
- **FRNG-008**: Stairway dimensions shown: minimum 36" clear width, 7-3/4" max riser, 10" min tread (CRC R311.7).
- **FRNG-009**: Guard height shown at open sides of stairs and landings ≥ 30" above floor below (minimum 36" at stairs, 42" at balconies > 30" per CRC R312).
- **FRNG-010**: Attic access opening shown (minimum 22" x 30" clear with 30" headroom per CRC R807.1).

---

## 4. Roof

- **ROOF-001**: Roof plan or framing plan provided showing slope, span, member sizes, and species/grade.
- **ROOF-002**: Roof slope identified on plan or in general notes.
- **ROOF-003**: Roofing material specified and rated for the applicable fire-hazard severity zone (FHSZ); Class A roofing required in WUI zones per CBC Section 707A.
- **ROOF-004**: Roof ventilation provided: 1/150 ratio (or 1/300 with baffles) per CRC R806.2; ridge/hip and eave vents shown.
- **ROOF-005**: Attic insulation specified with R-value meeting or exceeding CEC Title 24 Part 6 prescriptive requirement.
- **ROOF-006**: Eave and gable vents screened with 1/16" to 1/8" corrosion-resistant mesh per CBC Section 710A (WUI requirements).

---

## 5. Architectural / Egress

- **ARCH-001**: All rooms labeled with function and dimensions on floor plan.
- **ARCH-002**: Egress windows identified in all sleeping rooms with net clear opening dimensions shown; minimum 5.7 sq ft NCO, minimum 20" clear width, minimum 24" clear height, maximum 44" sill height AFF (CRC R310.2.1).
- **ARCH-003**: Emergency escape and rescue opening (EERO) sill height not to exceed 44" above finished floor without approved complying well (CRC R310.2.3).
- **ARCH-004**: Door schedule provided with door tag, size, fire rating, hardware, and swing direction; minimum 32" net clear width at egress doors (CRC R311.2).
- **ARCH-005**: Window schedule provided with tag, size, glazing type, U-factor, SHGC, and net clear opening (NCO) dimensions.
- **ARCH-006**: Exit discharge path from all exit doors to public way shown on site plan.
- **ARCH-007**: Smoke alarms shown in each sleeping room, outside each sleeping area, and on each story including basement per CRC R314.3.
- **ARCH-008**: Carbon monoxide alarms shown on each story with a sleeping area and within 15 ft of each sleeping room per CRC R315.3.
- **ARCH-009**: Stairway shown with code-compliant handrail at 34"–38" above nosing and graspable profile (CRC R311.7.8).
- **ARCH-010**: Bathroom shown with a minimum 21" clear floor space at water closet; 15" min from centerline to each side wall per CRC R307.

---

## 6. Mechanical (HVAC)

- **MECH-001**: Mechanical plan or HVAC equipment schedule provided showing equipment type, capacity, location, and manufacturer model.
- **MECH-002**: Heating and cooling equipment sizes supported by Manual J or CEC Title 24 Part 6 calculations.
- **MECH-003**: Duct routing shown or indicated in general notes; duct insulation specified meeting Title 24 Part 6 requirements.
- **MECH-004**: Combustion air provisions shown for fuel-burning equipment (CMC Section 701 or CRC M1702).
- **MECH-005**: Bathroom exhaust fan(s) shown with cfm rating ≥ 50 cfm for intermittent or ≥ 20 cfm for continuous per CRC M1507.3.
- **MECH-006**: Kitchen range hood or exhaust fan shown with duct to exterior (min 100 cfm intermittent) per CRC M1503.1.
- **MECH-007**: Whole-house mechanical ventilation system shown per CRC M1505/Title 24 Part 6 Section 150.0(o).
- **MECH-008**: Attic ventilation meets prescriptive requirements: minimum free area 1/150 ratio of attic floor area (CRC R806.2) unless higher bar applied by Title 24.
- **MECH-009**: Dryer exhaust duct shown routed to exterior with lint trap; maximum duct length per CMC Table 504.2.2.

---

## 7. Electrical

- **ELEC-001**: Electrical panel location and main service size (amperage) shown on plan.
- **ELEC-002**: Panel schedule provided or indicated on electrical plan.
- **ELEC-003**: All circuits labeled with load, breaker size, and wire gauge in panel schedule.
- **ELEC-004**: AFCI protection shown for all 120V 15A and 20A circuits in dwelling unit per CEC Article 210.12.
- **ELEC-005**: GFCI protection shown at all required locations (kitchen, bathrooms, garages, outdoors, crawl spaces, unfinished basements) per CEC Article 210.8.
- **ELEC-006**: Tamper-resistant receptacles specified in all locations per CEC 406.12.
- **ELEC-007**: Exterior lighting on automatic controls or occupancy sensors per Title 24 Part 6 Section 150.0(k).
- **ELEC-008**: EV-ready conduit or outlet provided in garage or at parking area per Title 24 Part 6 Section 150.0(w) (mandatory for new SFR).
- **ELEC-009**: Solar PV conduit and panel space reserved per Title 24 Part 6 Section 150.0(u) (mandatory for new SFR).
- **ELEC-010**: Smoke alarm interconnection method noted (hardwired with battery backup for new construction per CRC R314.4).

---

## 8. Plumbing

- **PLMB-001**: Plumbing plan or riser diagram provided showing fixture locations, drain, waste, and vent (DWV) routing.
- **PLMB-002**: Water heater location shown with seismic strapping detail (CPC Section 507.2); discharge pipe routed to approved location.
- **PLMB-003**: Water service size shown and supported by fixture count calculation per CPC Appendix A.
- **PLMB-004**: Fixture unit count provided and sized per CPC Table A 2.1.
- **PLMB-005**: Shower valves shown as pressure-balancing or thermostatic type per CPC Section 408.4 (max 120°F for bathtubs/showers).
- **PLMB-006**: Water service and meter location shown on site plan with point of connection to public main.
- **PLMB-007**: Backflow prevention device specified at hose bibbs and irrigation connections per CPC Section 603.
- **PLMB-008**: Gas line sizing calculation provided or referenced for all fuel-burning appliances.

---

## 9. Energy (Title 24 Part 6)

- **ENER-001**: Title 24 Part 6 compliance forms provided (CF1R — Certificate of Compliance; CF2R — Installation Certificate; CF3R — HERS Verification) or referenced by a HERS rater.
- **ENER-002**: CF1R signed and dated by licensed energy consultant or Title 24 software-generated.
- **ENER-003**: Insulation R-values specified in wall, floor/ceiling, and attic assemblies consistent with CF1R values.
- **ENER-004**: Fenestration U-factor and SHGC values for all windows and skylights consistent with CF1R and shown on window schedule.
- **ENER-005**: Cool roof or radiant barrier specified where required by CF1R compliance path.
- **ENER-006**: Water heater efficiency shown meeting or exceeding CEC Title 24 Part 6 Section 150.0(n) (heat pump water heater required for new SFR per 2022 code).
- **ENER-007**: Duct leakage testing required and noted on CF1R; HERS rater verification noted.
- **ENER-008**: Mandatory features summary in CF1R consistent with plans (lighting efficacy, solar zone, etc.).

---

## 10. Accessibility (CBC Chapter 11B — applies where Covered Multifamily not applicable; ADA Accessible Route for SFR additions triggering Chapter 11B)

- **ACCS-001**: Accessible route from public way or accessible parking to accessible entrance identified on site plan (CBC 11B-206.2.1).
- **ACCS-002**: At least one accessible entrance provided without steps; exterior accessible route slope ≤ 8.33% running, ≤ 2% cross-slope (CBC 11B-402).
- **ACCS-003**: If accessible parking is provided, stall dimensions shown: 9' wide + 5' access aisle (CBC 11B-502.2).
- **ACCS-004**: Interior accessible route width minimum 36" clear (CBC 11B-403.5.1).

---

## 11. Fire and Life Safety

- **FIRE-001**: Fire sprinkler system required for new SFR with aggregate floor area ≥ 5,000 sq ft in Santa Rosa (confirm with SR Municipal Code); NFPA 13D scope shown on plan.
- **FIRE-002**: Deferred submittal list provided for fire sprinkler system if applicable.
- **FIRE-003**: Fire-resistant construction details shown for attached garage separation: minimum 1/2" gypsum board on garage side of wall/ceiling per CRC R302.6.
- **FIRE-004**: Separation between dwelling and attached garage shown; door between garage and dwelling must be a 20-minute rated door or solid wood/solid steel (CRC R302.5.1).
- **FIRE-005**: Wildland-Urban Interface (WUI) Zone status noted; if in WUI zone, CBC Chapter 7A requirements applied (ignition-resistant construction, Class A roof, ember-resistant vents).

---

## 12. CALGreen (Title 24 Part 11)

- **CALG-001**: CALGreen checklist (Tier 1 or Tier 2) included with permit application per Title 24 Part 11 Section 301.1.
- **CALG-002**: Construction waste management plan or diversion statement included (minimum 65% diversion per CALGreen Section 4.408.1).
- **CALG-003**: Low-VOC adhesives, sealants, paints, and finishes noted in specifications per CALGreen Sections 4.504.2–4.504.3.
- **CALG-004**: Indoor moisture control measures noted: 3/4" hardboard or equivalent exterior wall sheathing moisture barrier; vapor retarder per CALGreen Section 4.505.2.
- **CALG-005**: Mechanical ventilation meeting CALGreen Section 4.506.1 (whole-house ventilation rate per ASHRAE 62.2 or CRC M1505).
- **CALG-006**: Water-conserving plumbing fixtures specified: toilets ≤ 1.28 gpf, lavatory faucets ≤ 1.2 gpm, shower heads ≤ 1.8 gpm per CALGreen Section 4.303.
- **CALG-007**: Electric vehicle charging infrastructure: provide panel capacity and raceway per CALGreen Section 4.106.4 (mandatory for new SFR).
- **CALG-008**: Drought-tolerant landscaping or MWELO compliant landscaping plan for areas ≥ 500 sq ft per CALGreen Section 4.304 (local water agency may impose stricter).
