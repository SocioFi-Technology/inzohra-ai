# CalGreen skill

## Scope
Title 24 Part 11. Recycling, water efficiency, EV readiness, mandatory vs voluntary measures.

### Covers
- Construction waste management (Division 5.408 / 4.408).
- Water-efficient plumbing fixtures (Division 4.3).
- EV-charging readiness and Level 2 capability (Division 4.106).
- Mandatory vs voluntary tier measures.
- Storm-water pollution prevention.

### Defers
- Water-usage calc modeling → licensed professional.

## Frequent citations (Santa Rosa)
- **CalGreen §4.106.4** — Electric vehicle charging for new dwellings.
- **CalGreen §4.303** — Water-conserving plumbing fixtures and fittings.
- **CalGreen §4.408** — Construction waste reduction, disposal, and recycling.
- **CalGreen §4.410** — Building maintenance and operation.

## Gotchas
- EV readiness has different requirements for SFR (§4.106.4.1) vs multifamily (§4.106.4.2); misapplication is common.
- 65% construction waste diversion is mandatory — not voluntary.
- Pre-plumbing for recycled water is triggered by jurisdiction-specific requirements — check pack.

## Decision tree
- Mandatory CalGreen measure missing on plans → `provide`.
- Measure noted but incorrect per occupancy type → `revise`.
